Challenges & Objectives

/ Project Overview

We modeled and visualized the eigensole-a smart insole equipped with sensors, circuits, and a battery-powered receiver case. The aim was to create high-fidelity 3D models that could be used in website showcases and product videos to communicate both form and function.

/ Challenges
  • Showcase both the aesthetic design and technical components of the eigensole through product visuals and animations.
  • Create sleek, minimal, and realistic renders that align with the brand tone.
  • Develop assets for use across website showcases, marketing campaigns, and investor presentations.
  • Communicate the product’s functionality effectively without needing a physical prototype.
/ Objectives
  • Highlight the core tech behind eigensole in a modern, digestible format
  • Leverage 3D models and Three.js to engage users visually
  • Ensure seamless performance across desktop and mobile

/ The Results

A sharp set of high-fidelity 3D renders and animations that bring the eigensole to life-highlighting its sleek design and smart functionality. These visuals became key in pitching the product’s vision without relying on a physical prototype.
