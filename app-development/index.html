App Development

A suite of engineering apps for eigenplus, designed to make complex calculations accessible, accurate, and intuitive. With a focus on clarity, precision, and interactivity, these apps help learners engage with core concepts through clean interfaces, visual tools, and real-time results.

Challenges & Objectives

/ Project Overview

We partnered with eigenplus to design a suite of mobile apps that simplify core engineering concepts through intuitive interfaces and real-time interactivity. From concrete mix calculators to Mohr’s Circle visualizers, each app is crafted to turn complex calculations into hands-on learning experiences.

/ Challenges

We needed to translate dense technical formulas into clean, mobile-friendly tools. The challenge was to maintain engineering accuracy while ensuring clarity, usability, and consistency across all apps.

/ Objectives
  • Design user-friendly interfaces for technical workflows
  • Ensure consistency across 5+ apps
  • Visualize complex concepts like Mohr’s Circle and RCC design
  • Optimize for performance and accessibility on Android devices
/ Final Outcome

The result is a collection of high-impact engineering apps with over 500,000+ downloads and 1,000+ five-star reviews, helping learners and professionals engage with structural concepts through intuitive design and interactive visuals—built entirely by eigenstudio.
